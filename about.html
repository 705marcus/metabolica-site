The People Behind Metabolica

About Us.

Built by a family with decades of clinical experience, legal precision, and a conviction that oncology nutrition has been underserved for too long.

Dr. Steve Rallis

Founder & Chief Medical Officer

DC, ND  ·  Integrative Oncology

Clinician, educator, and the
architect of Metabolica’s formula.

Dr. Steve Rallis is an integrative oncology practitioner focused on metabolic approaches to cancer care. As Chief Medical Officer of The DRIPBaR Canada, he has worked with thousands of patients navigating complex chronic illness and cancer treatment, with a focus on improving resilience, recovery, and quality of life.

Dual clinical doctorates in chiropractic (DC) and naturopathic medicine (ND) — a rare combination that allows him to approach complex disease from both structural and metabolic perspectives.

His clinical work centres on high-dose IV vitamin C, metabolic therapies, and precision nutrition strategies designed to support patients without unintentionally fueling disease processes. He is the author of When Oranges Become Apples: What Your Oncologist Won’t Tell You About IV Vitamin C and Cancer and a frequent speaker at international medical conferences.

Metabolica was born directly from his clinical experience: seeing patients who needed better nutritional tools, but had none designed for their biology.

Nick Rallis

Operations, Legal & Finance

Queen’s Commerce & Queen’s Law  ·  Bennett Jones

Building Metabolica with
legal precision and operational discipline.

Nick Rallis brings a dual background in Queen’s University Commerce and Queen’s University Faculty of Law, giving him a rare combination of financial, legal, and operational fluency. He works at the intersection of structure and execution, ensuring that Metabolica is built to scale within a complex regulatory environment.

Currently with Bennett Jones — one of Canada’s leading business law firms — where he is exposed to complex transactions, regulatory frameworks, and high-level corporate strategy.

He has led coordination across legal counsel, formulation teams, and manufacturing partners, translating strategy into concrete action. His strengths lie in financial modelling, regulatory alignment, and operational discipline, bringing clarity and order to an otherwise complex build.

Marcus Rallis

Brand, Growth & A.I.

University of Waterloo  ·  Global Business & Digital Arts

Translating clinical science into
a brand that practitioners trust.

Marcus Rallis is completing his undergraduate degree at the University of Waterloo in Global Business and Digital Arts, where he has developed a multidisciplinary approach to building modern brands.

He leads branding, design, and AI-driven systems at Metabolica — shaping how the company communicates, scales, and operates.

From visual identity to digital infrastructure, his focus is on creating a brand that is both clinically credible and commercially compelling. He drives partnerships with clinics, builds distribution channels, and develops the company’s digital presence.

His work ensures that complex scientific ideas are translated into clear, compelling narratives that resonate with practitioners, patients, and investors.
